How to Delete My Avite Account

This page is for Avite.

Steps to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account within the app by following these steps:

  • Open the Avite app
  • Click on your name in the top left corner of the "My" page
  • Enter the "Settings" page
  • Click "Delete Account" to proceed with deletion

Data Deletion and Retention Policy

After account deletion, we will:

  • Permanently delete your personal information (such as email and username)
  • Permanently delete all your data records
  • Delete all associated device records
  • Retain necessary payment and transaction records for legal or payment compliance reasons for up to 180 days
